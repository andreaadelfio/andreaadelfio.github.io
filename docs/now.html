Ph.D. in Astrophysics

I'm currently a Ph.D. student in Multimessenger Astrophysics at DESY and Julius-Maximilians-Universität Würzburg, based in Berlin, Germany.

The focus of my research is the analysis of neutrino and gamma-ray data from Blazar jets to understand particle acceleration and emission processes in these extreme environments.
